Frequently asked questions
Transport modes explained
Barge (Inland Waterway)
Best for heavy cargo on Rhine, Danube, Meuse and Belgian waterways. Slowest but cheapest and lowest CO₂. Ideal for non-urgent bulk freight.
Rail (Freight Train)
Best balance of speed, cost and sustainability for medium-to-long distances. Serves major European corridors. Departure times are fixed — plan ahead.
Road (HGV / Truck)
Fastest door-to-door. Highest CO₂ and cost. Best for urgent, short-distance or final-mile delivery. Multiple departure slots daily.
