Why join
Built for operators, not just shippers
CorridorBook isn't a load board. It's a slot marketplace that fills your capacity with pre-qualified, payment-verified cargo.
Your own operator dashboard
Manage your available slots, view incoming bookings, update departure schedules, and see capacity utilisation — all in one place.
Fill empty capacity
Empty barge runs and underloaded trains are a known problem in the hinterland corridor. CorridorBook routes demand directly to your available slots before departure.
Verified shippers only
All shippers on CorridorBook are KVK/VAT verified businesses. Payment is held by the platform and released on gate-in confirmation. No payment risk.
Real-time slot management
Push available slots via API or our web interface. Update gate-open times, cut-off times and capacity in real-time. Changes propagate to shippers instantly.
Performance analytics
Monthly reports on fill rate, revenue per TEU, CO₂ performance, and route popularity. Benchmark yourself against the corridor average.
No integration required to start
You can start with manual slot entry via the operator web portal. API integration (REST + webhook) is available when you're ready to automate — full docs provided.

Technical integration
Connect your TMS or booking system
Push slots and receive bookings via our REST API. Webhooks notify your system the moment a booking is confirmed.
POST a new slot
Push available departure slots from your TMS directly into the CorridorBook marketplace. Slots appear in search results in real-time.
POST /api/v1/slots

{
"operator_id": "rhenus-nl",
"mode": "barge",
"origin": "RTM",
"destination": "ANR",
"depart": "2026-05-21T06:00Z",
"capacity_teu": 12,
"price_eur": 310
}
Booking webhook
When a shipper confirms a booking on your slot, CorridorBook fires a webhook to your endpoint with full booking details in milliseconds.
POST https://your-tms.com/webhooks/cb

{
"event": "booking.confirmed",
"ref": "MBP-4QR7W2",
"slot_id": "slot_rtm_anr_0521",
"cargo_kg": 24000,
"containers": ["1 × 40HC"],
"payment_status": "held"
}
